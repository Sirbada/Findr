📋 Brand Guidelines

✅ Do's

  • Maintain minimum clear space around logo
  • Use provided color variants for different backgrounds
  • Scale proportionally - never distort
  • Use monochrome versions for single-color applications

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't change colors outside approved palette
  • Don't add unauthorized effects or shadows
  • Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast
  • Don't use below minimum size (120px width for full logo)
